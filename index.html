Why this matters now

Over the past decade and across 50 years of growth, SoC has become a global leader in computing education and research. Yet for many students, the path to excellence is still blocked by financial strain.

  • About one in five students — roughly 1,200 — need financial support.
  • 1,200 students are forced to choose between studies and financial pressure.
  • Existing financial aid is meaningful, but still not enough to meet current needs.

Excellence should not be a matter of personal wealth.

What the bursary makes possible

The 50th Anniversary Bursary Fund helps remove financial barriers, restore students' time for learning and growth, and build a legacy of care from one generation to the next.

Donations are endowed, so every contribution supports students in perpetuity. Your gift does more than solve today's need — it strengthens access for decades to come.

Tax deduction for qualifying donors

For Singapore tax residents with qualifying donations and reference numbers, tax deductions may apply at 2.5 times the donated amount.
